# Orbit Down — 24 Second Product Film

## Style

Use `design.md` exactly: `#090B15`, `#0E1220`, `#141827`, `#F7F4F1`, `#AAA9B6`, `#FF7358`, `#F4B44A`, `#43C9C1`, `#8BD6A0`, and `#1A0B08`. Inter 800 leads; Inter 400 supports. Mood: warm, modern, precise, personal, premium.

## Rhythm

`HOOK — build — PRODUCT PEAK — breathe — CTA`

Five scenes across 24 seconds. The opening makes anticipation emotional; scene two proves the product; scene three expands across Apple screens; scene four gives the privacy promise room to breathe; scene five closes with a concise invitation.

## Global rules

- Primary transition: 0.65 second blur crossfade with `power2.inOut`.
- Accent transition: centered circle iris for the cross-device reveal.
- Keep two to five background decoratives per scene: orbit rings, a moving satellite, radial glow, hairline arc, or faint grid.
- Every scene element enters. Background elements breathe or orbit with finite repeats only.
- Keep the dominant headline at 88 pixels or larger and body copy at 25 pixels or larger.
- Product imagery is a focal plane, not a background texture.

## Beats

1. **Anticipation, 0.0–4.7:** Coral glow, two orbit rings, icon, label, and “The moments ahead deserve to be seen.” Rings expand, satellite arcs, icon settles, type rises. Blur crossfade at 4.1.
2. **One beautiful place, 4.4–9.6:** Faint grid, teal glow, large Mac product plane, compact title, and three feature tokens. Window lifts; title cascades; tokens pop. Circle iris at 8.8.
3. **Every screen, 9.3–14.8:** iPhone, iPad, and Mac fan into one composition with “Every screen. Same feeling.” Platform labels cascade and an accent rule draws. Blur crossfade at 13.6.
4. **Privacy breathe, 14.5–19.7:** Mint glow, concentric protected orbit, and three promises: No ads, No tracking, No account. End on “Your moments stay yours.” Blur crossfade at 18.5.
5. **CTA, 19.4–24.0:** Icon, warm glow, two arcs, “Make it worth the wait,” platform line, and Free · Private · Ad-free promise. Final fade at 23.4.

## Recurring motifs

Orbit rings communicate time and continuity. Coral signals the emotional hook; teal and mint signal trust; amber marks anticipation. Device surfaces share soft shadow and 24–36 pixel corners.

## Negative prompt

No gradient text, neon purple-blue wash, generic equal card grids, tiny screenshots, jump cuts, infinite loops, random movement, confetti, glass overload, or stock imagery.
